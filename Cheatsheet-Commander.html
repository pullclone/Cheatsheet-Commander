ğŸ” Boolean Operators

Narrows results by requiring all terms. Use to connect distinct ideas.

OR

Broadens results by allowing any listed terms. Use for synonyms or related phrases.

Excludes specific terms from results. Use to filter out irrelevant contexts.

Example: "digital rights" NOT copyright
" " (Quotes)

Searches for an exact phrase in that word order. Increases precision.
